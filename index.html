Master's Degree (Energy Engineering)

Feasibility of Micro Nuclear Reactors in desalination plants (Master's Thesis)

I studied the possibility of using Micro Modular Reactors (1-20 MW) to power Reverse Osmosis desalination plants, together with renwables sources. I developed an optimization model in Python to determine the optimal design and managment of the energy system, aiming to minimize costs or CO2 emissions.

Emissions Analysis of a Combined-Cycle Power Plant

I estimated CO2, NOx and CO emissions of the Napoli Levante Combined-Cycle Power Plant (Tirreno Power) and compared the calculated values with the plant’s Environmental Declaration and Integrated Environmental Authorization (AIA).

2D Numerical Simulation of Microtab Effects on a Wind Turbine Airfoil

A course mate and I investigated the effect of a microtab on a wind turbine airfoil. We performed CFD analysis with ANSYS Fluent to estimate the increase in the overall sound pressure level.
